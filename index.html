About Me

Born on June 8th, 1990, in Kalaburagi, Karnataka, I come from an agricultural family where my father's teaching profession sparked my passion for science, art, and culture. This unique blend led me from rural roots to aerospace excellence.

Currently serving as Senior Lead Aerospace Engineer at Collins Aerospace (Raytheon Technologies), I previously spent 5 years as Senior Research Scientist at DRDO, contributing to indigenous defense systems like AEW&C and SAR for UAV.

Beyond engineering, I'm a 2D aviation artist creating detailed handmade aircraft models. Since 2011, I've been conducting India's first volunteer aerospace awareness campaign, reaching 8,000+ students across Karnataka government schools through aircraft demonstrations and exhibitions.

My mission is simple: inspire young minds to pursue aerospace innovation through the perfect fusion of technical expertise and artistic expression, contributing to India's Make in India and Atmanirbhar Bharat vision.

Upcoming Events

Stay updated with my latest school visits, aircraft exhibitions, and aerospace awareness programs across Karnataka and beyond. These events feature live aircraft demonstrations, detailed 2D model exhibitions, and interactive sessions designed to spark interest in aerospace engineering and defense technology among students and aviation enthusiasts.

Whether you're an educator looking to inspire your students, a young aspiring engineer, or simply passionate about Indian aerospace achievements, these events offer unique opportunities to explore the fascinating world of aviation through art and engineering.

Join Hands With Me

Together, let's inspire India's future aerospace innovators

Join me in this noble mission to reach every corner of India with aerospace awareness. Whether you're an educational institution, corporate sponsor, government organization, or individual supporter, your partnership can help us inspire thousands more students to pursue careers in aerospace engineering and defense technology.

Together, we can expand this grassroots movement to remote schools, create more detailed aircraft models, organize larger exhibitions, and develop innovative educational materials. Your support directly contributes to nurturing young minds who will design India's next-generation aircraft, satellites, and defense systems.

Let's unite to make aerospace education accessible to every child, regardless of their background, and build the foundation for India's technological supremacy in the skies and beyond.

GJC Kadugudi, Government High School, SOUTH4, Bengaluru

🚀 Pradeep’s Aerospace and Defence Awareness Campaign

6th Outreach – Academic Year: 2025–2026

📅 Date: 19th July 2025

🏫 Venue: GJC Kadugudi, Government High School, SOUTH4, Bengaluru

With the continued blessings, support, and guidance of my mentors and well-wishers, I’m happy to share the successful completion of the 6th Aerospace and Defence Awareness Campaign for the academic year 2025–2026.

This initiative remains committed to nurturing interest and awareness among government school students about India's growing capabilities in aerospace and defence technologies.

🛩️ Special Talk Theme:

“Indigenous Aircraft Demonstration Activity”
Followed by an Exhibition of Handmade 2D Aircraft Models with Micro-Detailing

Aircraft Models Showcased:

  • ✈️ LCA Tejas – The Journey & Tejas Mk1A
  • ✈️ Tejas Mk2
  • ✈️ AMCA (Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft)
  • ✈️ DRDO NETRA AEW&C
  • ➕ Other key indigenous defence platforms

📌 Session Highlights:

  • ⏱️ Duration: 3 hours 35 minutes (8:45 AM – 12:15 PM)
  • 👨‍🎓 Beneficiaries: 488 students (Only High School – Classes 8 to 10)
  • 🎨 Special Focus: Showcasing India’s indigenous aerospace achievements through 2D aviation art and model displays
  • 💬 Q&A Session:
    • 45-minute interactive session
    • Over 20+ thought-provoking questions from students, reflecting deep interest and engagement

The passion, enthusiasm, and curiosity displayed by the students reaffirm the value of these grassroots outreach initiatives. It is truly heartening to see young minds connect with India’s aerospace ambitions in such meaningful ways.

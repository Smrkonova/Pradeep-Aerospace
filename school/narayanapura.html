Narayanapura Government High School, Bengaluru

🚀 Pradeep’s Aerospace and Defence Awareness Campaign

4th Outreach – Academic Year: 2025–2026

📅 Date: 12th July 2025

🏫 Venue: B Narayanapura Government High School, Bengaluru

With the blessings and guidance of my mentors and well-wishers, I am pleased to share the successful completion of the 4th Aerospace and Defence Awareness Campaign for the academic year 2025–2026. This campaign continues to inspire and engage young minds through interactive sessions focused on India’s aerospace journey and indigenous defence technologies.

🛩️ Special Talk Theme:

“Indigenous Aircraft Demonstration Activity”
Followed by an Exhibition of Handmade 2D Aircraft Models with Intricate Micro-Detailing

Featured Aircraft Models Included:

  • ✈️ LCA Tejas – The Journey
  • ✈️ Tejas Mk2
  • ✈️ AMCA (Advanced Medium Combat Aircraft)
  • ✈️ DRDO NETRA AEW&C
  • ➕ And several other indigenous defence platforms

📌 Session Highlights:

  • ⏱️ Duration: 2 hours 45 minutes (9:00 AM to 11:45 AM)
  • 👨‍🎓 Beneficiaries: 385 students
    • Primary + High School students (Classes 8 to 10)
  • 🎨 Special Focus: 2D Aircraft Art Exhibition showcasing India’s indigenous aerospace capabilities
  • 💬 Q&A Session:
    • 45-minute interactive discussion
    • Over 30+ insightful questions asked by curious and engaged students

The enthusiasm, energy, and curiosity shown by the students truly reflect the impact of such awareness campaigns. These sessions are a small yet meaningful step towards inspiring future scientists, engineers, and innovators in the field of aerospace and defence.

Personal Journey

From Agriculture Roots to Aerospace Heights

The Beginning

Born on June 8th, 1990, in Kalaburagi (Gulbarga), Karnataka, Pradeep's journey began in an agricultural family where his father's teaching profession sparked his passion for science, nature, art, and culture. This unique blend of technical expertise and artistic vision would later define his mission to inspire India's youth.

Educational Milestones:

  • Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science (2012) - VTU Karnataka
  • Master of Technology in Computer Network Engineering (2014) - VTU Karnataka
  • Drawing Grade Exams: Lower & Higher Grades with Distinction (FCD)
  • Project Internship at LRDE, DRDO during M.Tech

Artist Profile

Where Engineering Meets Art

Since 2011, attending Aero Shows and Defence Expos ignited a new vision - creating aerospace-themed art that showcases India's technological prowess and inspires future generations.

Artistic Journey: Started with cultural events and art competitions during school days, securing multiple medals and certificates. Completed Drawing Grade Exams - "Lower Grade: Paper 1-6" and "Higher Grade: Paper 1 to 6" with distinction (FCD) from Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board.

Signature Masterpiece

"DRDO@60 - DRDO in my EYE, DRDO - Make In India"

Dimensions: 34″ × 27″

Medium: Coloured Pencil and Acrylic Colour

Theme: Celebrating 60 years of DRDO's journey in service of the Nation (1958-2018)

Artistic Vision:

  • LEFT PART: National Flag depicting Digital India (Saffron), Make in India (White), and Swachh Bharat (Green) - the dream projects of Hon'ble PM Narendra Modi.
  • CENTRAL PART: Trees representing DRDO with emphasis on Women's Empowerment. The eyes of women visualize advanced indigenous products. Roots represent all 29 states of India forming DRDO.
  • RIGHT PART: Indigenous achievements - AEW&C systems, 4th+ generation fighters, ballistic missiles, Main Battle Tanks showcasing India as one of the elite nations in aerospace and defence technology.

Philosophy & Vision

Art as a Bridge to Inspiration

"My aim is to promote avionics art to the younger generation so they can contribute and take it further with new innovations. Every detailed stroke in my aircraft models represents the precision and dedication required in aerospace engineering. Through art, I want to show students that science and creativity can work hand in hand to build a stronger, more self-reliant India."

- Pradeep Laxman Savalkar
